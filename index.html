Important Operational Notes

Large-scale construction activities such as slab filling, lantern work, and raw material stacking, beams are not included in standard charges and will be billed separately based on actual work requirements. Rental materials and heavy equipment are also excluded from any subscription or fixed plan. Charges for these items will be calculated based on the project area, duration of equipment usage, and volume of materials handled.

All major construction-related costs—including raw material filling, rental material usage, labor uplift charges, and loading or unloading logistics—will be evaluated by the site manager . Pricing will be finalized according to on-site conditions and actual execution needs, ensuring transparency and accuracy in billing with Rate. CAWR (charge according to work rates).
